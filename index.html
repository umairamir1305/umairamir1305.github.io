About

I'm a mechanical engineering student focused on structural and fluid systems analysis — the kind of work that sits between hand-calculated theory and simulation: applying classical failure criteria, running FEA, and validating results against analytical solutions.

My projects so far center on three recurring problems in mechanical design: how pressure vessels fail under thermal and structural load, how fluid distributes itself through a branching pipe network, and how a landing gear strut behaves under static and aerodynamic loading. Each one pairs a MATLAB-based analytical model with hand-verified engineering theory.
