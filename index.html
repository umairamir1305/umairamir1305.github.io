01 Featured project

Aircraft landing gear strut analysis

A simplified oleo-pneumatic main landing gear strut, modeled in SolidWorks 2023 and put through static finite element analysis and an aerodynamic flow simulation. Loading conditions were set up and checked in MATLAB before I trusted the FEA output.

Landing gear takes the highest loads an airframe sees on the ground. It is a good test of whether a stress result from a solver actually holds up against a hand calculation, or needs a second look.

03 More projects
02 · CFD

GT car spoiler: CFD simulation analysis

A CFD study of external airflow over a GT car rear spoiler, run in SimScale on its OpenFOAM backend. Used simpleFoam with a k-omega SST turbulence model to solve the pressure and velocity fields at two inlet speeds on a 2.1 million cell hex-dominant mesh.

Outcome: compared results at 10 m/s and 25 m/s to check whether down-force scaled with velocity squared, as the theory predicts.

03 · MATLAB

Pressure vessel simulation

Thermal and structural behavior of a thick-walled pressure vessel, simulated in MATLAB using Lame's equations. Failure was evaluated against the Von Mises criterion across a range of wall thicknesses and internal pressures.

Outcome: a parametric safety analysis showing how safety factor shifts with wall thickness and pressure, instead of a single-point answer.

04 · MATLAB

Pipeline network flow analyzer

A MATLAB simulation of a 7-pipe hydraulic distribution network, solved with the Hardy-Cross method to converge on flow distribution and head loss across a branching, looped system.

Outcome: a converged flow and head loss solution across every branch, without needing to solve the full system of equations by hand.
